Privacy Policy – Cave Map

Thank you for using Cave Map! This privacy policy explains how Cave Map handles your data. We are committed to protecting your privacy. Cave Map does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

1. Data Collection

Cave Map does not collect, process, or share any personal data. No user accounts, login information, or identifiers are used. The app functions entirely on your device.

2. Location Access

Cave Map does not access your device’s location. The map view is centered manually by the user. No GPS, background location, or location-based services are used.

3. Map and Cave Data

Cave Map uses the following data sources:

  • Apple Maps for the interactive map display. The map is rendered by Apple’s MapKit framework, subject to Apple’s Privacy Policy.
  • OpenStreetMap cave locations, retrieved using the Overpass API operated by maps.mail.ru. OpenStreetMap's Privacy Policy applies.

No personal or device-related data is shared with any of these services by Cave Map.

4. Offline Use

The app stores retrieved cave data locally on your device for offline access. This includes cave names and geographic coordinates. No user data or usage history is stored or transmitted.

5. Navigation Feature

If you tap on a cave and choose "Navigate", the app opens Apple Maps with the selected destination. This is handled through system functionality. No location data is transmitted by the app itself.

6. Analytics and Tracking

Cave Map does not use analytics, crash reporting, advertising, or any tracking technologies.

7. Children’s Privacy

Cave Map is suitable for all ages and does not collect personal information from any users, including children under the age of 13. There are no in-app purchases, advertisements, or user accounts.

9. Changes to This Policy

Any future updates to Cave Map's behavior will be reflected in this privacy policy. Currently, no personal data is collected or shared, and that commitment will be maintained.

Summary

Cave Map is a privacy-focused app. It does not collect or store any personal data, does not access your location, and uses only public, open data sources for cave information.
