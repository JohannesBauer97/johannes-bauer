The processing of your location data is based on your explicit consent in accordance with Art. 6(1)(a) of the GDPR. Consent is obtained via the iOS system permission prompt. No further legal basis is required for this runtime-only, on-device processing.

  • Data portability (Art. 20 GDPR)
